Our Sweet Story

A journey of passion, creativity, and unwavering commitment to culinary excellence

The Beginning

Founded in 2015 by award-winning pastry chef Shruti Raj, RSCakery Bliss began as a dream to create extraordinary desserts that tell stories through taste. What started in a small kitchen has grown into a premier destination for luxury confections.

Our Philosophy

We believe that every dessert should be a work of art. Using only the finest Belgian chocolates, Madagascar vanilla, and locally sourced organic ingredients, we craft each piece with meticulous attention to detail and boundless creativity.

Meet Our Master Chef

SR

Shruti Raj

Executive Pastry Chef & Founder

With over 15 years of international experience and training from Le Cordon Bleu Paris, Chef Shruti brings French techniques and innovative creativity to every creation. Her award-winning designs have been featured in top culinary magazines worldwide.
